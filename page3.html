Inception

Inception is a mind-bending thriller that dives into the world of dreams. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. His unique ability has made him a coveted player in the world of corporate espionage, but it has also cost him everything he loves.

Cobb is offered a chance at redemption when he is given the seemingly impossible task of planting an idea in someone's mind, a process known as inception. If he succeeds, it will be the perfect crime, but a dangerous enemy anticipates Cobb's every move.

Starring

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Ellen Page as Ariadne
